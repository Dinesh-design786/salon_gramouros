# 🏆 GlamourOS: 7-Step Hackathon Presentation Runway Script

This script outlines the narrative, visual transitions, and key talking points to pitch **GlamourOS** to retail-tech judges. Use the integrated **Runway Controller** at the top of the dashboard to automate the tab navigation.

---

## 🎭 The Narrative Hook
*“Indian salon chains operate in a chaotic environment. Staff bookings clash, high-value customers churn silently, hair dyes run dry mid-treatment, and calculating complex stylist commissions takes hours of manual sheet auditing. GlamourOS solves this with an Omnichannel AI Operating System.”*

---

## 🏃 The 7-Step Presentation Runway

```carousel
### Step 1: The Problem & Live Alerts
*   **Visual action:** Show the **Live Notifications** sidebar.
*   **Narrative:** *"Right off the bat, GlamourOS detects that L'Oreal Hair Dye has dropped to 4 units at Banjara Hills, below our safety buffer. Sandeep Naidu is operating at a high 85% capacity. The manager has immediate logistical awareness before their shift begins."*

<!-- slide -->

### Step 2: The Roster Calendar
*   **Visual action:** Navigate to the **Operations Calendar** tab. Show the clean timeline columns.
*   **Narrative:** *"This is our daily operations calendar. It splits time grids by clocked-in therapists. In Banjara Hills, we see Kavya, Sandeep, and Rohan Sharma managing highly packed agendas. The system displays a live branch-load active price tag (Surge / Happy hour factors)."*

<!-- slide -->

### Step 3: WhatsApp Inquiry
*   **Visual action:** Switch to the **WhatsApp Assistant** tab. Click prompt #2: *"Jubilee Hills haircut at 3:30 PM"*.
*   **Narrative:** *"Now let's simulate a customer booking inquiry. Ananya Reddy texts our WhatsApp chatbot in conversational Hinglish: 'Sandeep ke sath afternoon 3:30 PM slot haircut double-book check conflict'."*

<!-- slide -->

### Step 4 & 5: AI Scheduler Overrides Conflict
*   **Visual action:** Watch the AI WhatsApp agent reply: *"Slot Conflict Resolved! Shifted reservation by 45 minutes to 3:45 PM."* Switch back to the *Operations Calendar* tab. Note a new **purple cell** with a **Sparkles badge** under Sandeep's column.
*   **Narrative:** *"Our AI Scheduling Engine parsed the text, matched Sandeep's specialty, detected that he was double-booked at 3:30, and auto-adjusted the time slot to 3:45 PM to protect staff and avoid queues. The calendar grid immediately updated with our purple WhatsApp-sourced booking."*

<!-- slide -->

### Step 6: Smart POS Cashless Settle
*   **Visual action:** Navigate to the **POS Terminal** tab. Choose *Priyanka Verma* from unbilled appointments. Add retentive coupon `COMFORT15` (triggered by the pink Churn Warning alert!). Click *Settle Invoice* and scan the UPI QR code. Click *Download PDF Receipt*.
*   **Narrative:** *"We now checkout Priyanka. Our POS detects that she is a high churn risk. We apply coupon COMFORT15 suggested by our AI Advisor, split standard 18% Indian GST (SAC 999721), and render a cashless UPI QR code. Settle invoice auto-deducts our stock count, updates the commission ledger, and lets us download a PDF tax invoice."*

<!-- slide -->

### Step 7: Executive Analytics & Forecasting
*   **Visual action:** Navigate to the **Executive Analytics** tab. Show the responsive Recharts.
*   **Narrative:** *"Finally, as a chain owner, I open Executive Analytics. Recharts maps weekly revenue performance, daily branch peak occupancy grids, and loyalty tier splits. Crucially, the AI Logistical advisor recommends ordering +16 hair dyes today to clear the weekend treatment surge. Operations, logistics, and payroll are fully connected."*
```

*“GlamourOS isn’t a student wireframe. It’s a production-ready, fully connected platform combining Express APIs, Socket.IO updates, Recharts dashboards, and PostgreSQL cloud triggers, proving how AI and automated workflows can revolutionize the ₹50,000 Crore Indian retail beauty industry today.”*
